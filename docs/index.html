Instant transcription
Hold ⌃⇧Space, speak, release. Whisper transcribes your voice in under a second and pastes it wherever your cursor is.
AI cleanup
Ollama runs locally to fix grammar, punctuation, and capitalisation as you dictate. No API key. No data sent anywhere.
Works everywhere
Slack, Notion, Gmail, Linear, Terminal — Soll pastes into any app using Accessibility. No integrations needed.
Truly private
Everything — Whisper, Ollama, your dictionary — runs on your Mac. Your words never touch a server.
✦ Skills

Voice-triggered AI actions

Say a trigger phrase and Soll sends your speech to a local AI model to do something smart — summarise, rewrite, translate, and more.

skill summarise this
Summarise
Speak what you're looking at and get a crisp, bulleted summary back at your cursor.
Output →
"Here's a 3-point summary: 1. The meeting covered Q3 targets… 2. Action items were assigned to…"
skill write a reply
Write a reply
Dictate the gist of what you want to say. Soll drafts a polished reply ready to send.
Output →
"Hi Sarah, thanks for the update! I've reviewed the proposal and I think we should…"
skill make it formal
Make it formal
Speak casual thoughts, get back polished professional prose — perfect for emails and docs.
Output →
"I would like to follow up on our previous discussion regarding the project timeline…"
skill translate to Spanish
Translate
Speak in English, get instant output in any language. Works with any language Ollama supports.
Output →
"Hola equipo, quería compartir una actualización rápida sobre el proyecto…"

🔒 Privacy-first

Your voice stays on your Mac

Most voice tools send your audio to remote servers. Soll doesn't. Every part of the pipeline — transcription, AI cleanup, your dictionary — runs locally. Always.

🎙️
Whisper runs on-device
OpenAI's Whisper model is downloaded once and runs entirely in Metal on your Mac. No API key needed.
🤖
Ollama stays local
AI cleanup uses Ollama running on your machine. Your text never leaves your device for processing.
📖
No account, ever
No login, no telemetry, no subscription. Soll is free software — use it for as long as you want.
